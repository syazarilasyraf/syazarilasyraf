About

I studied Electronic (Communication) Engineering, but soon realized programming is what I love. I care about using both skillsets to make an impact in areas like mental health or education. I live and work by my values—integrity, inclusion, radical candor and a people-first approach. I focus on the impact and ethics of my work; trying to leave the world a better place than I found it.
